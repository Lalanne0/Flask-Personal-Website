Creating a web app to automate pentests.

Along with seven other students, I carried out a project for a French digital services company. In this project, we used various technologies, such as Flask, HTML/CSS/JS, Bash, as well as Python scripts to create a seamless interface allowing users to launch a simple network attack without coding knowledge.

Designing a capacitive load cell.

I thought about how to make lifts safer, and designed a sensor to achieve this. I used pure electronics, assembly, beam mechanics, as well as 3D modelling and prototyping to conduct my experiments.

Creating a manual decision tree classifier.

I manually implemented a decision tree to classify edible and poisonous species of mushrooms based on their characteristics. I then compared the performances of the tree based on the splitting criterion used to create it (Gini impurity or entropy).

Discovering AI with Scikit-learn.

I took my first steps in AI using the MNIST dataset. I explored different possibilities for optimising learning and then developed a neural network to compare the results.
